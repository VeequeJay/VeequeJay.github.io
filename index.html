About Me

Victor Oyindamola Julius is a versatile data science and financial analytics professional with a strong background in machine learning engineering, stochastic modeling, and financial derivatives pricing. With expertise in Python, Power BI, and advanced data analytics, Victor has worked on complex financial projects, including developing Monte Carlo simulations, trinomial and binomial tree models, and applying machine learning algorithms to real-world financial data.

Currently, Victor is pursuing an MSc in Financial Engineering, he is applying cutting-edge models like the Heston and Bates models for pricing options and calibrating interest rate models using the CIR model. Victor has also worked on a diverse range of projects, including creating automated financial reporting systems in Power BI, building machine learning-powered financial chatbots, and designing interactive portfolio websites.

In addition to his technical expertise, Victor is passionate about using technology to solve real-world challenges, such as improving access to healthcare for financially constrained individuals. His goal is to bridge the gap between finance, technology, and social impact.
